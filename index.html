about me

I am a software engineer with over 5 years of experience building responsive web and mobile applications. I love building applications with React & React Native because it enables me to create multiple types of applications within a similar tech stack. I enjoy working with small & medium-sized businesses because of the passion they tend to have for their work. Want to learn more about me? Let's talk!

Making the Intangible Tangible

You have the vision & I'll sweat the details to bring that vision to life. A pleasurable user experience isn't just about flashy animations; it's about an intuitive end-to-end experience. It's about making sure that in the short time someone is on your site, there is a positive impression that leads them to bring their friends back next time.

Transparent Timelines

Every project has its own unique challenges. I take a structured & detailed approach to building your application from day one, while using industry-standard product management tools to ensure everyone involved is in the loop & that the timelines are open & honest.

TypeScript is Home

TypeScript is a well-versed and powerful superset to JavaScript. TypeScript also pairs seamlessly with my favorite frontend technologies, React & React Native. Once I unlocked the benefits of TypeScript, it was hard to imagine going back to a non-typed language.

Node.js is My Rock

As comfortable as I am with the frontend, I am equally competent with backend technologies such as Node.js, Firebase, and MongoDB. When it comes to SQL databases, I prefer to work with PostgreSQL & pair it with my favorite ORM, Sequelize.
